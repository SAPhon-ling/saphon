feat: p t̪ʙ t k ʔ m n ɸ s w j ɾ i ʏ e a o 
note: Popky lists /f/ and /h/ as phonemes and does not list the phoneme /ɸ/. Birchall has clarifed that these three sounds are allophones of a single phoneme /ɸ/. Birchall also notes that there is not evidence that the mid front round vowel /ø/ found in Popky is contrastive.
