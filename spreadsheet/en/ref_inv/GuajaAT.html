Citation

Cunha, Péricles. 1987. Análise fonêmica preliminar da língua guajá. UNICAMP.

Summary

Cunha proposes underlying Ṽ and N. The author proposes 5 phonological rules: (1) optional post oralization of N preceding oral V; (2) nasalization of R when following or preceding nasal Ṽ (described as "nasal environment"); (3) leftward LDNH triggered by stressed root final nasal Ṽ, with V and R as undergoers and obstruents as blockers; (4) LN of V when preceding nasal N; (5) LN of vowels following word initial N

Notes

The following is including by the author on where fieldwork was conducted: "Em 1980, em companhia do Prof. Mércio Pereira Gomes, fui ao Posto de Atração da FUNAI, nos Cocais do Alto Turiaçu onde, entre os meses de fevereiro e maio, tive dois períodos de pesquisa, um de 22 e outro de 25 dias, junto aos Guajá alli establecidos" (p. 2 -4). Two degrees of vowel nasalization (unclear how to code), with strong nasal vowels appearing in stressed syllables in root-final position, and the weak nasal vowels appearing to the left of the strong nasal vowel by virtue of LDNH (p. 38-39).
